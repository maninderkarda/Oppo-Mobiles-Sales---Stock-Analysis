# Oppo-Mobiles-Sales---Stock-Analysis
This Power BI project was developed for OPPO, one of the world’s leading smartphone brands known for its innovation in mobile technology, strong retail distribution network, and growing presence across India. Founded in 2004, OPPO operates in more than 60 countries and focuses on delivering smart devices with advanced camera technology and user-centric experiences.

The dashboard was designed to help business stakeholders monitor sales performance and distributor stock availability across Punjab. It provides year-over-year sales comparisons at region, city, ZSM, ASM, and distributor levels, along with detailed inventory tracking by mobile model. The report enables decision-makers to identify high-performing regions, monitor stock coverage, track distributor sales trends, and optimize inventory planning using interactive Power BI visuals and filters.
